Rum Balls

  • Sweetened condensed milk

  • Rum or rum essence
Method:

Banana Bread

Bake Whole cake or slice

Banana Bread

Ingredients:
  • Ripe bananas
  • Flour, baking soda, salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs & vanilla
Method:
  1. Mash bananas.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Fold in bananas and dry ingredients.
  4. Bake until a skewer comes out clean.

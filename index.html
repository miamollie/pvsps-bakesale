Rum Balls

Ingredients:
  • Crushed biscuits
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Shredded coconut
  • Rum or rum essence
Method:
  1. Crush biscuits into fine crumbs.
  2. Mix with cocoa, condensed milk, and rum.
  3. Shape into balls and roll in coconut.
  4. Chill before serving.

Oatmeal Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Ingredients:
  • Rolled oats
  • Flour, baking soda, salt
  • Butter & sugar
  • Eggs & vanilla
  • Chocolate chunks
Method:
  1. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Stir in dry ingredients, oats, and chocolate.
  4. Scoop dough, bake until golden edges form.

Bake Make Ahead

Banana Bread

Ingredients:
  • Ripe bananas
  • Flour, baking soda, salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs & vanilla
Method:
  1. Mash bananas.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Fold in bananas and dry ingredients.
  4. Bake until a skewer comes out clean.
